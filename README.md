# Pong Game Documentation

## Overview
Pong is one of the earliest arcade video games, and it simulates table tennis. Players control paddles to hit a ball back and forth, aiming to score points by getting the ball past the opponent's paddle.

## Features
- Two-player mode (local)
- AI opponent in single-player mode
- Score tracking
- Adjustable difficulty levels
- Sound effects

## How to Play
1. Start the game.
2. Choose between single-player or two-player mode.
3. Control your paddle to hit the ball back towards your opponent.
4. Score by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
5. The first player to reach a specified score wins the game.

## Controls
### Single Player
- Move Paddle Up: `W`
- Move Paddle Down: `S`

### Two Player
- Player 1 Move Paddle Up: `W`
- Player 1 Move Paddle Down: `S`
- Player 2 Move Paddle Up: `Up Arrow`
- Player 2 Move Paddle Down: `Down Arrow`

## Game Mechanics
- The ball resets to the center after each point.
- Each paddle can hit the ball to change its direction.
- The game ends when a player reaches the target score, usually set to 10 points.
